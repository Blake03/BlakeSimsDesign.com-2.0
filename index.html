My Works

LeilaAlexandra.com

LeilaAlexandra.com is a fully responsive single-page art gallery that I built from scratch using HTML5/CSS3, jQuery, and PhotoShop. It features a Lightbox gallery, smooth scroll navigation, show/hide button, and an animated back top button. It uses the power of HTML5-Boilerplate, Normalize.css, and Modernizr to ensure that it looks beautiful on any possible combination of browsers and devices.

Who is Blake

I am a self-taught web/graphic designer with a passion for learning. Back in 2005, I started designing websites and updating content with WordPress, Dreamweaver, and Photoshop for a small web hosting company. Due to a lack of clients, I began working construction jobs in addition to my work online. Soon I found myself with less and less time to spend growing my skills as a developer. Eventually, I came to the realization that I was heading down the wrong path.


About a year ago I decided it was time to get back to doing what I love. I devoted every second of my spare time to learning everything I could about the latest technologies being used in modern web design. I then narrowed my focus to learning as much as possible about Responsive Design, HTML5/CSS3, jQuery, PhotoShop CC, and GitHub.


Every day I wake up before around 4 or 5 a.m. and begin writing code, solving problems, and sharpening my skills. I am having a great time building upon what I have learned and growing as a web designer. Lately, I have been learning more about using Linux, Sass, Compass, Susy, Flexbox, and Underscores.


When I'm not working I enjoy spending time in nature, exercising, cooking healthy & delicious meals, listening to music & podcasts, or watching tutorial videos and documentaries.


If you have any questions or would like to work together, you can reach me at Blake@edelta.net
